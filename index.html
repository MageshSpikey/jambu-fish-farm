About Us

Welcome to Jambu Fish Farm – we sell only live and fresh fish directly from our farm since 2015. Our fish are raised with the utmost care using natural feeding methods and caught using traditional net techniques to ensure the best taste and texture.

Natural Feeding: Vegetable scraps, green grass, cooked rice

Traditional Catching: Net methods without harming fish

Result: Excellent taste and structure

Fish Available

Varal Fish (Snake Head)

Taste: Mild, slightly sweet flavor

Kannadi (Rohu)

Taste: Very less fat, absorbs spices well

Eri Vaval (Red Pomfret)

Taste: Rich umami flavour with firm texture

Pillu Kandai (Grass Carp)

Taste: Mild sweet with umami flavor
